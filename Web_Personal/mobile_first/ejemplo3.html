PORTFOLIO

POSTER AMERICAN HORROR STORY: TANGLED

This was a poster i had to design for an hypothetic movie or TV show (in my case a new season of an existent one) in the subject of “Language and Digital Techniques”. The objective was to make a poster only with Adobe Illustrator and using “flat design” as inspiration.

CALL TO ACTION POSTER

This poster was made for Basic Projects subject. Each of us had to choose one ODS and make a call to action poster related with some of the objectives that ODS has. In my case I had chosen the number 9 which speaks about reducing the inequalities, so I decided to choose the poverty as my topic.

Taking the statistics which are in the official website of ODS and in their reference links I created a proportion which represents graphicaly the amount of people in extreme poverty compared with the ones who lives wihout economic issues. The final message is really clear 9 out of 10 people is poor.

DUST JACKETS

This project was made for Editorial Projects subject and it consisted in designing 3 dust jackets for a book trilogy only employing creative typography. In my case I have chosen the maze runner saga. We had to layout the summary and the texts inside the book flaps and its back cover too.

PICTOLIFE

This work made for Information Design Projects, was about creating a group of pictograms, with the intention of representing the life of a famous character that you admire. In my case I have chosen “La Veneno” a famous spanish transexual vedette well known in Spain because of her particular appearences at some TV shows. I thought she was a good option because she represented LGBTQ+ community in a time where they didn´t have recognition and representation.

It was in 2021 when she became internationally famous thanks to a TV show about her life called “Veneno” produced by a famous spanish producers couple “Los Javis” (Javier Calvo and Javier Ambrossi).

MATTE PAINTING

Matte painting is a technique used usually in the film and animation industry to create digital images or scenes that represent landscapes, environments, or visual elements that would be difficult or expensive to film in real life. It involves painting or creating digital images on separate layers to compose a final scene.

In this case in the Digital Illustration class we had to take different kind of photos from a Free Bank Images webpage, trying that they had a similar illumination and create a new scene which seems as a real one. To accomplish this we had to change the colors, illumination, levels, curves, add some noise to the image...
